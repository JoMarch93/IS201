5 Cool Facts about Singapore

  1. Singapore is both a city and a country, making it unique as the world's only island city-state
  2. Home to the world's first night zoo, allowing visitors to observe nocturnal animals in their natural habitat.
  3. Chewing gum is prohibited in Singapore.
  4. Singapore is recognized as one of the world's greenest cities.
    • They have plants growing on buildings to help with heat control and asthetics.
  5. Home to the world's tallest indoor waterfall at Jewel Changi Airport.
